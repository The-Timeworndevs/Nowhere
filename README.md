# Nowhere: The Plane of the Lost
___
#### Nowhere, a desolate prison engineered to kill you by ancient necromancers, a place you should not be...

Nowhere is a massive mod aiming to bring a narrative that I, and many others, have been building for many, *many* years. Set on the desolate wasteland called Nowhere, your goal is to survive, thrive, and defeat the forces imprisoned here long ago, while also uncovering the past of this world.

At first progression will feel like a more difficult version of vanilla progression, with a theme of resource scarcity, but as you progress, you will quickly discover there is far more than meets the eye; new weapon types, resources, machines, ancient artifacts, and even a story waiting to unfold...
